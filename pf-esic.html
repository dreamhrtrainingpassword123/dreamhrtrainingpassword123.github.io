PF & ESIC Compliance Services

PF (Provident Fund) and ESIC (Employee State Insurance) compliance is mandatory for eligible businesses in India. Non-compliance can lead to heavy penalties, legal notices and operational risks. Managing PF & ESIC requires accuracy, expertise and timely execution.

At Dream HR Consultancy, we provide complete end-to-end PF & ESIC compliance services ensuring your business remains fully compliant, risk-free and professionally managed.

Our PF & ESIC Services Include

  • Issuance of Certificate of Coverage from EPFO
  • Preparation and submission of statutory registers and returns
  • Employee PF transfer, withdrawal and pension claim support
  • ESI benefits assistance including medical and accident claims
  • Employee helpdesk support for PF & ESIC queries
  • Handling inspections by statutory authorities
  • Representation in quasi-judicial cases (7A, 7Q, 14B, 45A, etc.)
  • Coordination with EPFO & ESIC departments
