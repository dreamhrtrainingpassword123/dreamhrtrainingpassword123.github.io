PF & ESIC Compliance Services

The objectives of the Employees Provident Fund Act, 1952 and Employees State Insurance Act, 1948 are to provide social security benefits to employees and industrial workers. Even a minor calculation error or delay in compliance may lead to employee disputes, legal notices, and heavy financial penalties.

Dream HR Consultancy is professionally equipped to manage compliance for PAN India locations and become your trusted strategic partner in corporate governance, payroll discipline, and statutory compliance management.
