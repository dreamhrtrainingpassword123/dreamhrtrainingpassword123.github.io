PF ESI Inspection

Employees State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) is a multifaceted governmental structure designed to give much-needed socioeconomic protection to Indian workers. The ESI system seeks to provide a variety of advantages to the Indian working population.

ESI inspection ensures employee benefits and labor law compliance. It helps detect hidden employment and verifies records properly.

Advantages of the ESI Scheme

The ESI plan addresses employees’ health and wellness concerns. Employees’ health care expenses are covered by this plan.

  • Complete medical care
  • Compensation for lost wages
  • Family coverage

Inspection Methodology

Authorities verify salary registers, challans, and employee records to ensure compliance.
