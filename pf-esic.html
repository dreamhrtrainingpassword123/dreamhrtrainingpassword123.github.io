PF & ESIC Compliance

Employees State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) is a multifaceted governmental structure designed to give much-needed socioeconomic protection to Indian workers.

ESI inspection ensures employee benefits and labor law compliance. It helps detect hidden employment and verifies records properly.

Advantages of the ESI Scheme

  • Complete medical care
  • Compensation for lost wages
  • Family coverage
  • Monetary & non-monetary benefits

Types of Benefits

  • Medical Benefits
  • Disability Compensation
  • Sickness Benefits
  • Maternity Benefits
  • Unemployment Allowance
  • Dependents Benefits

Inspection Process

  • Salary register verification
  • Contribution checking
  • Expense verification
  • Compliance validation
