Overview

PF (Provident Fund) and ESIC (Employee State Insurance) compliance is one of the most critical legal requirements for businesses in India. Every eligible organization must ensure proper registration, timely filings, and accurate employee benefit management.

At Dream HR Consultancy, we provide end-to-end PF & ESIC compliance services ensuring that your organization stays fully compliant while protecting employee benefits and avoiding legal risks.

Why PF & ESIC Compliance is Important?

  • Avoid heavy penalties, interest (7Q) and damages (14B)
  • Ensure social security benefits for employees
  • Maintain legal safety and avoid inspections issues
  • Build trust and credibility with employees
  • Mandatory requirement under Indian labour laws

Challenges Faced by Companies

  • Incorrect PF/ESIC calculations
  • Delay in monthly filings and payments
  • Handling employee claims and transfers
  • Managing inspections and notices
  • Lack of updated knowledge of compliance rules
