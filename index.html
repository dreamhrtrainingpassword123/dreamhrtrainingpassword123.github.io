Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers To Common Questions

Is PF Registration Mandatory?

Yes. Subject to the applicability provisions under the EPF & MP Act, eligible establishments are required to obtain PF registration and comply with monthly filings.

Can You Handle Complete Payroll?

Yes. We manage salary processing, payslips, statutory deductions, TDS coordination, bonus, leave and full & final settlement.

Do You Provide PAN India Services?

Yes. We provide online HR, Payroll, PF, ESIC, Labour Law and Recruitment services across India.

Can You Help During Labour Inspections?

Yes. We assist clients in documentation, statutory registers, compliance review and inspection support.
