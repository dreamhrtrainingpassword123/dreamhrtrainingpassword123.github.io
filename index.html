Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about PF, ESIC, Payroll, Labour Law Compliance, Recruitment and Statutory Compliance Services offered by Dream HR Consultancy.

Who needs PF Registration?

Any establishment covered under the EPF Act is required to obtain PF Registration. Our experts assist with registration, monthly compliance, UAN, KYC, ECR filing and complete EPFO support.

Do you provide complete Payroll Management?

Yes. We provide complete payroll processing including salary calculation, payslips, attendance, leave management, statutory deductions, bonus, F&F settlement and compliance reporting.

Do you provide services across India?

Yes. Dream HR Consultancy provides PAN India HR, Payroll, Recruitment, PF, ESIC, Labour Law Compliance, Factory Act Compliance, Licensing and Statutory Compliance services through both online and onsite support.
